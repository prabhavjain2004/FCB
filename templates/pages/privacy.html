How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Booking Management: Process and manage your gaming slot reservations
  • Communication: Send booking confirmations, updates, and notifications
  • Payment Processing: Securely process payments via Razorpay
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage to enhance our platform
  • Customer Support: Provide assistance and resolve issues
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory requirements

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
