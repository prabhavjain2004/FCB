Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation and refund terms

Last Updated: November 1, 2025

Important: Strict No-Refund Policy

Due to the nature of our gaming slot booking service, all bookings are final and non-refundable. Please read this policy carefully before making a booking.

Policy Overview

TapNex Gaming Cafe operates a strict no-refund policy for all gaming slot bookings made through our platform at forge.tapnex.tech.

Once a booking is confirmed and payment is processed, it cannot be cancelled, modified, or refunded under any circumstances.

Why This Policy?

This policy exists for the following reasons:

  • Slot Reservation: When you book a slot, we reserve it exclusively for you, preventing others from booking
  • Resource Allocation: We prepare gaming stations and resources based on confirmed bookings
  • Lost Revenue: Last-minute cancellations result in revenue loss as slots cannot be re-booked
  • Operational Efficiency: This ensures smooth operations and fair access for all users

No Exceptions

We cannot process refunds or cancellations for any reason, including but not limited to:

Late Arrival / No-Show

Failure to arrive on time

Change of Plans

Personal schedule conflicts

Technical Issues

Internet or personal device problems

Third-Party Issues

Friend cancellations or group changes

Book Responsibly

Before confirming your booking, please ensure:

  • You have confirmed your availability for the selected date and time
  • You have reviewed the game, duration, and pricing details
  • You understand that the booking is final and non-refundable
  • You can arrive 15 minutes early to your scheduled slot time

Payment Issues

If you experience payment processing errors:

Double Charge Protection

If you're charged multiple times for the same booking due to a technical error, contact us immediately at info@tapnex.tech with proof of duplicate charges. We'll investigate and issue refunds for verified duplicate transactions only.

This only applies to genuine technical errors, not multiple separate bookings.

Platform Fees - Non-Refundable

Important: Platform Fees Are Always Non-Refundable

Platform fees are non-refundable under all circumstances, including booking cancellations, no-shows, or any other reason. This policy applies even in cases where the booking amount itself might be refunded due to exceptional circumstances.

What are Platform Fees?

  • Platform fees are charges applied to cover service maintenance, technology infrastructure, payment processing, and operational costs
  • These fees are clearly displayed during the checkout process before payment confirmation
  • Platform fees may vary based on booking amount and cafe policies
  • By completing your booking, you acknowledge and accept that platform fees are non-refundable

Example: If you book a slot for ₹500 with a ₹50 platform fee (total ₹550), even if the ₹500 booking amount is refunded due to exceptional circumstances, the ₹50 platform fee will not be refunded.

Exceptional Circumstances

Refunds may be considered ONLY in these specific cases:

  • Cafe Closure: If our cafe is unexpectedly closed during your booking time
  • Service Unavailable: If the booked game/service is not available due to our fault
  • Major Technical Failure: If our systems completely fail preventing service delivery
