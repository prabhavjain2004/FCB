Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I book a gaming slot?
Simply browse available games, select your preferred date and time slot, choose between private or shared gaming, and complete the payment. You'll receive instant confirmation!
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major payment methods through Razorpay including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?
Yes! You can cancel or modify bookings from your dashboard. Please check our refund policy for details on cancellation timelines and refund eligibility.
What if I'm late for my booking?
We recommend arriving on time to get your full gaming session. Late arrivals may result in reduced gaming time. If you're running late, please contact support immediately.
What's the difference between private and shared gaming?
Private gaming gives you exclusive access to the gaming station, while shared gaming allows multiple players to book the same slot at a discounted rate. Choose based on your preference!
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely! We use Razorpay's secure payment gateway with industry-standard encryption. We never store your card details on our servers.
